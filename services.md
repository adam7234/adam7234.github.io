## Installs & assembly
- TV mounting (stud / masonry where appropriate)
- Shelving, curtain rods, blinds
- Furniture assembly (IKEA and non-IKEA)
- Hanging mirrors, frames, heavy items

## Repairs & patching
- Drywall patching (small to medium)
- Door alignment, latches, handles
- Caulk + minor water sealing
- Loose fixtures, squeaks, small adjustments

## Punch-list help
If you have 6–12 small tasks, this is usually the best value. Send the list and photos.

## What I don’t do
High-voltage electrical work, major plumbing, gas work, structural changes. If it’s unsafe or requires a licensed trade, I’ll tell you directly and help you scope it.
